Anatoli Boukreev

One of the world's best, and most unknown mountaineers

Here is a list of just a few of Anatoli Boukreev's life moments and accomplishments!

  • 1958 - Anatoli was born in Korkino, part of the USSR.

  • 1987 - His first solo Ascent was of Lenin Peak. A 7000 meter massif located on the border of Tajikistan and Kyrgyzstan

  • 1989 - Anatoli helped to establisha new route on Kangchenjunga, a famous Himalayan Peak

  • 1990 - Anatoli climbed Denali's west rib route solo

  • 1996 - He was responsible for savng the lives of several mountaineers on Mount Everest during one of the worst mountaineering disasters ever.

  • 1998 - Anatoli was killed while attemting to climb the south face of Annapurna. He claimed to have a had a premontion in a dream that and avalanche would kill him. That is how he died.
